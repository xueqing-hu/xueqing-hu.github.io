Research

My research uses hyperspectral imaging, RGB imaging, interpretable machine learning, and hygrothermal modelling to study bio-colonisation and moisture-related deterioration on heritage surfaces.

The broader goal is to develop robust and practical workflows for detecting façade bio-colonisation, interpreting its environmental and material drivers, and supporting long-term condition assessment of historic buildings.
