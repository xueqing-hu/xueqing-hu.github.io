I am a PhD researcher at Ghent University, affiliated with the Building Physics research group and IPI-URC-imec. My research focuses on the identification and prediction of deterioration in historical buildings and heritage.

Education

  • PhD, Ghent University, Belgium

  • MSc, Southeast University, China

Research

The PhD project focuses on biological deterioration on heritage façades. The project combines UAV-based imaging, hyperspectral imaging, building physics simulation, and machine learning to detect real deterioration patterns, explain their physical drivers, and improve risk prediction models.
